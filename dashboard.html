🧠 What is the RL Agent?
🤖

The Reinforcement Learning agent is an AI trained to find wasteful AWS resources. It works like a game — it gets rewarded when it finds real savings, and penalized when it makes bad calls. Over time it gets smarter.

📐
Algorithm: PPO (Proximal Policy Optimization). Chosen over DQN because it's more stable and doesn't make wild policy changes when AWS costs fluctuate.
🎯
Reward formula: Score = (cost_saved × 0.6) − (risk × 0.3) − (disruption × 0.1). Prioritizes savings while penalizing risky or disruptive actions.
🔄
Feedback loop: When you approve or reject a recommendation, the agent gets a +1 or −1 reward and retrains. It literally learns from your decisions.
